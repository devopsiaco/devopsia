🔐 Devopsia Privacy Policy

At Devopsia, your data’s not just numbers in a table — it’s a responsibility.
This policy lays out how we collect, use, and guard your info like a well-secured kube cluster.

📦 What We Collect

When you register or interact with the platform, we may collect things like:
• Your email address (no spam, we promise)
• Profile and usage details
• Logs that help us debug, improve, and sometimes just marvel at our system’s uptime

🧠 How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:
• Make Devopsia smarter, faster, and more helpful
• Communicate updates, support info, and the occasional product love note
• Keep the platform secure and running like a well-oiled CI/CD pipeline

🍪 Cookies & Techy Snacks

We might drop cookies (not the edible kind) into your browser to:
• Improve your experience
• Remember preferences
• Understand how you use Devopsia so we can make it better
You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings — or disable them entirely if you like living on the edge.

🇪🇺 Your Rights (GDPR Squad)

If you’re hanging out in the European Union, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) has your back.
You have the right to:
• Access your personal data
• Correct inaccurate info
• Request that we delete your data (aka the "rage quit" button)
Just contact us — we’ll handle your request with respect and speed (but no, not via carrier pigeon).

🗃️ Data Retention

We hold on to your personal data only as long as necessary:
• To keep the platform running
• To comply with legal obligations
• Or until our logs say, "Time to purge"

🔄 Policy Updates

We iterate, just like your deployment pipeline. So this Privacy Policy may change from time to time.
When we make meaningful changes, we’ll post the updated version here — no fine print tricks.
